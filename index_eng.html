About Us

"The Streets of Nablus: Our Stories" is an innovative digital platform bridging the rich history and culture of Nablus with modern technology. This initiative aims to document the stories and experiences embedded in every corner of the city through barcode symbols placed in various areas. Visitors and residents can scan these barcodes to explore historical and cultural information associated with each location, enriching the visitor experience and raising awareness about the city's heritage.



Our Missions

Developing Our City

We help to introduce the areas of our city through QR codes spread in Nablus areas (coming soon). When scanned, it takes you to a screen containing information about the location, photos, and map.
